Position: Research Assistant Professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong

Department/ Unit: Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages

Closing date: 31 Dec 2024

The Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages is young and vibrant, devoted to the study of language and mind, with strengths in theoretical linguistics, language acquisition, bilingualism, sign linguistics, and language disorders. We are seeking applications for a Research Assistant Professorship. The Research Assistant Professor will primarily be responsible for working on projects related to neurocognitive modeling of language learning and processing led by Prof. Gangyi Feng. Candidates should have a strong background in Neurolinguistics and/or Psycholinguistics, as well as a solid quantitative experience in neuroimaging, machine learning, language intervention, and/or large language models.

Applicants should have (i) PhD in Linguistics, Psychology, Cognitive Neuroscience, Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field; (ii) postdoctoral research experience and/or a strong journal publication record commensurate with rank; and (iii) computer programming experience in MATLAB, Python, C++, or other languages. Prior experience with fNIRS, EEG, and/or fMRI research is highly advantageous.

The successful candidate is expected to (a) conduct research as the main duty; (b) actively pursue external research grants, such as General Research Fund, in the capacity of Principal Investigator and Co-Investigator; (c) co-supervise and mentor students at undergraduate and graduate levels; and (d) undertake minimal teaching duties at undergraduate and graduate levels.

The appointment will normally be made on a contract basis for up to three years. Applicants should submit an online application here and attach a full resume, a list of representative publications, and a cover letter, together with names and e-mail addresses of three referees to whom the applicants' consent has been given for their providing references. Queries can be sent to Prof. Gangyi Feng (g [DOT] feng [AT] cuhk [DOT] edu [DOT] hk).

The Chinese University of Hong Kong is ranked among the top 50 universities worldwide according to QS World University Rankings. Situated in a metropolis that offers a multitude of living possibilities, with three-quarters of Hong Kong comprised of countryside, the university provides an excellent environment for academic and personal growth.

香港中文大学研究助理教授招聘公告

部门：语言学与现代语言系

截止日期：2024年12月31日

语言学与现代语言系是一个充满活力的新兴部门，专注于语言与思维的研究，具有理论语言学、语言习得、双语、手语语言学和语言障碍等领域的优势。我们正在招聘研究助理教授。研究助理教授将主要负责参与冯刚毅教授领导的语言学习与处理的神经认知建模相关项目。候选人应具备神经语言学和/或心理语言学的扎实背景，以及在神经影像学、机器学习、语言干预和/或大语言模型方面的丰富定量经验。

申请人应具备：(i) 语言学、心理学、认知神经科学、工程学、计算机科学、统计学或相关领域的博士学位；(ii) 博士后研究经验和/或与职位相称的优秀期刊发表记录；(iii) MATLAB、Python、C++ 或其他语言的计算机编程经验。具有 fNIRS、EEG 和/或 fMRI 研究经验者尤佳。

成功的候选人预计将：(a) 以研究为主要职责；(b) 以首席研究员或联合研究员的身份积极争取外部研究资助，例如一般研究基金；(c) 共同监督和指导本科生和研究生；(d) 承担少量本科和研究生教学工作。

任命通常以合同形式进行，期限最长为三年。申请人应在此处提交线上申请，并附上完整的简历、代表性出版物列表和求职信，以及三位推荐人的姓名和电子邮件地址（申请人应同意由该推荐人提供推荐信）。如有疑问可联系冯刚毅教授 (g [DOT] feng [AT] cuhk [DOT] edu [DOT] hk)。

根据 QS 世界大学排名，香港中文大学位列全球大学前 50 名。大学坐落在提供多种生活可能性的大都市，香港面的四分之三区域是郊野公园，为学术和个人成长提供了良好的环境。
