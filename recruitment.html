Position: Research Assistant Professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong

Department/ Unit: Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages

Closing date: 31 Dec 2024

The Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages at The Chinese University of Hong Kong is seeking applications for a Research Assistant Professorship in the field of language learning and processing. Candidates should have a strong background in Neurolinguistics and/or Psycholinguistics, as well as a solid quantitative experience in machine learning, large language models, language intervention, and/or neuroimaging.

Applicants should have (i) PhD in Linguistics, Psychology, Cognitive Neuroscience, Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, or a related field; (ii) postdoctoral research experience and/or a strong journal publication record commensurate with rank; and (iii) strong computer programming (MATLAB, Python, C++, etc.) or statistics background. Prior experience with fNIRS, EEG, and/or fMRI research is highly advantageous.

The successful candidate is expected to (a) conduct research as the main duty; (b) actively pursue external research grants, such as General Research Fund, in the capacity of Principal Investigator and Co-Investigator; (c) co-supervise and mentor students at undergraduate and graduate levels; and (d) undertake minimal teaching duties at undergraduate and graduate levels. The appointee will work closely with Prof. Gangyi Feng’s team in the department.

Appointment will normally be made on a contract basis for up to three years. Applicants should submit an online application here and attach a full resume, a list of publications, and a cover letter, together with names and e-mail addresses of three referees to whom the applicants' consent has been given for their providing references. The review of applications will begin immediately and will continue until the position is filled. Only shortlisted applicants will be notified. Queries can be sent to Prof. Gangyi Feng (g [DOT] feng [AT] cuhk [DOT] edu [DOT] hk).

The Chinese University of Hong Kong is ranked among the top 50 universities worldwide according to QS World University Rankings. Situated in a metropolis that offers a multitude of living possibilities, with three-quarters of Hong Kong comprised of countryside, the university provides an excellent environment for academic and personal growth.

香港中文大学研究助理教授招聘公告

部门：语言学与现代语言系

截止日期：2024年12月31日

香港中文大学语言学及现代语言系现诚聘语言学习与处理领域的助理教授研究职位。候选人应具有深厚的神经语言学和/或心理语言学背景，以及在机器学习、大型语言模型、语言干预和/或神经影像学方面丰富的定量经验。

申请者应具备：(i) 语言学、心理学、认知神经科学、工程学、计算机科学、统计学或相关领域的博士学位；(ii) 博士后研究经验和/或与职级相称的良好期刊发表记录；(iii) 较强的计算机编程（MATLAB、Python、C++ 等）或统计学背景。具有 fNIRS、EEG 和/或 fMRI 研究经验者更佳。

成功应聘者应：(a) 以开展研究为主要职责；(b) 以首席研究员和联合研究员的身份积极争取外部研究基金（例如General Research Fund）；(c) 联合指导本科生和研究生；(d) 承担本科生和研究生的最低限度教学任务。受聘者将与冯刚毅教授在该系的团队密切合作。

任命通常以合同形式进行，合同期限最长为三年。申请人应在此处提交线上申请，并附上完整的简历、出版物清单和求职信，以及三位推荐人的姓名和电子邮件地址（申请人应同意由该推荐人提供推荐信）。申请审查工作将立即开始，并将持续到该职位被填补为止。只有入围的申请人才会收到通知。如有疑问，请联系冯刚毅教授 (g [DOT] feng [AT] cuhk [DOT] edu [DOT] hk)。

根据 QS 世界大学排名，香港中文大学位列全球大学前 50 名。大学坐落在提供多种生活可能性的大都市，香港面的四分之三区域是郊野公园，为学术和个人成长提供了良好的环境。
