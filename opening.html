Postdoc positions

We are currently seeking to recruit two postdoctoral researchers in the field of language cognitive neuroscience. We expect applicants with the following Qualifications:

  • Hold a PhD degree in Psychology, Linguistics, Cognitive or Computational Neuroscience, Language and Hearing Science, Computer Science, Biomedical Engineering, or related fields.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.
  • Experience and expertise in collecting and analyzing neuroimaging data (fMRI, fNIRS, EEG/MEG).
  • Strong programming skills (MATLAB, Python, C++), and solid foundation in statistics.
  • Candidates with experience in neuroimaging techniques, multivariate pattern analysis (MVPA), and expertise in large language models or neural network modeling will be given preference .
If you are interested, please send your CV to the lab director, Prof. Gangyi Feng (g.feng@cuhk.edu.hk). The salary will be determined based on the applicant's experience and university regulations.

Prospective students

Students in Linguistics, Psychology, Education, Computer Science, Statistics, Engineering, or a related major are welcome to contact Prof. Feng for Ph.D. program application.
